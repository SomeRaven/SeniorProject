Need Help?

How-To

Check In Students

Go to the Check In page, type or scan the student number, and click Check In.

View or Edit Student Info

Head to the Students page. You can search, edit, or remove students right there.

Manage Classes

Go to the Classes page to add, edit, or remove classes. You can also assign or remove students from classes directly in each class panel.

Export Data

Navigate to Export, select your filters, and choose your format: CSV or JSON.
